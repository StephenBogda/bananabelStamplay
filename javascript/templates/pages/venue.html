ABOUT

This unique venue features extraordinary views of Fifth Avenue and St. Patrick’s Cathedral from a manicured historic garden, complete with a reflective pool and fountain. The private, customizable indoor and outdoor space accommodates 165 guests for a reception and 120 for a seated meal.

Guests may experience the outdoor garden in any season; a tented section of the garden is offered year-round. The interior space is flooded with natural light from wall-to-wall windows overlooking the Prometheus statue, Rockefeller Plaza and the marquee of Radio City Music Hall.
